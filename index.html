Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

Is GG Giveaways free to use?

Yes! GG Giveaways is completely free to use with all features available to everyone. There are no premium tiers or hidden costs.

How does the activity tracking work?

GG Giveaways tracks member activity by monitoring messages sent in your server. To be considered active, a member must have sent at least 10 messages in the last 30 days, been active on at least 3 different days, and not triggered the spam detection.

What permissions does the bot need?

GG Giveaways needs permissions to read and send messages, read message history, add reactions, and manage messages. When you add the bot to your server, these permissions will be automatically requested.

Can I customize the giveaway appearance?

Currently, the appearance of giveaway embeds is standardized. However, you can customize the prize, duration, winner count, and role requirements for each giveaway you create.

How do I set up role requirements?

When creating a giveaway, you can use the 'role' parameter to specify a required role or 'minimum_role' parameter to set a minimum role hierarchy level. Users without these roles won't be able to enter the giveaway.

Is there a limit to how many giveaways I can run?

There's no hard limit on the number of giveaways you can run simultaneously. However, to maintain bot performance, we recommend keeping active giveaways to a reasonable number.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

GG Giveaways collects the following information when you use our Discord bot:

  • Discord server IDs
  • User IDs of members who interact with the bot
  • Message activity data for determining active users
  • Giveaway entry and participation data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide giveaway functionality within your Discord server
  • Track user activity to determine eligibility for activity-gated giveaways
  • Generate server-specific analytics about engagement
  • Improve the bot's functionality and performance

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely and is only accessible to the bot's processes. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access.

4. Data Retention

Activity data is retained for 30 days by default. Completed giveaway data may be stored for up to 90 days for reroll functionality.

5. Your Rights

Server administrators can request deletion of their server's data at any time by contacting us through our e-mail support@gg-giveaways.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our e-mail support@gg-giveaways.com.
